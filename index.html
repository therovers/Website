Study beyond Travels and Destinations

Who are we

Rovers: A NGO founded to facilitate learning, exploring and experiencing of different cultures and heritage. The organisation started with an initiative to educate students about the culture and heritage of India through the mode of study programmes and practical exposure. India is vast, diverse and the oldest thriving civilization so, our study programmes aspire to make travel, learning and living parallel processes to stimulate growth in an individual developing a sense of pride. We want to make travel a mode of educating instead of leisure by engaging students in group workshops, individual activities which would bring out the best in each.
