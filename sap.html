The Rovers : An NGO founded to facilitate learning, exploring and experiencing different cultures and heritage. This organisation has been started with an initiative to educate students about the culture and heritage of India through the mode of study programmes and practical exposure. Our study programmes aspire to make ‘travel’, ‘learning’ and ‘living’ parallel processes to stimulate growth in an individual & develop a sense of pride. We want to make travel a mode of education instead of leisure by engaging students in practical learning through individual activities & group workshops which bring out the best in each
